FAQs

Q: IS ALDERTUNE A CONTINUATION OF DELTARUNE/UNDERTALE?

A: No, Aldertune is it's own tale, seperate from both Undertale and Deltarune. Despite having settings and characters that coincide to both tales, Aldertune's main setting is NOT in the realm of Deltarune or Undertale.

Q: IS SANS IN ALDERTUNE?

A: Yes, Sans is in Aldertune, but not a main character, or a character we'd see often. Like Papyrus, his appearence is a cameo.

Q: HOW OLD IS THE MAIN CAST OF ALDERTUNE?

A: Aldertune's main cast ranges from 17 to 21 year olds. Most, if not all of the main characters are adults, and should be treated that way.
